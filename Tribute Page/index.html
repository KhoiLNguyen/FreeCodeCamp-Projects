Sung Jin-Woo

Sung Jin-Woo (성진우成秦禹) is the protagonist of the series Solo Leveling. He used to be known as the World's Weakest Hunter and is on his way to becoming the World's Strongest Hunter after he Reawakened and became a Player.

Appearance

Before his re-awakening, Jin-Woo was a young man of average height with short black hair and grey eyes; his build was frail and despite being considered stronger than a normal human, he was the weakest among Hunters. After his Reawakening as a Player, Jin-Woo was able to grow stronger by increasing his stats, which led to a sudden change to his whole stature/appearance. Because of this, he grew a few inches taller, his facial features became a lot sharper and he gained a lean/muscular build. His whole demeanor also became more serious and intimidating.

Personality

Prior to Reawakening, Jin-Woo was a kind, nervous, and very timid young man. He respected his fellow Hunters and was brave enough to continue fighting in Dungeons in order to earn money to care for his sister and to pay for his mother's medical bills. Jin-Woo is also shown to be an intelligent person, being able to stay calm under pressure as he figured out the clues to fighting the boss of the Double Dungeon while most of the experienced hunters in their party panicked and were killed by the statues when they were trying to escape. After being Reawakened he strives to become a stronger hunter than before. Because of this, Jin-Woo became focused on getting his daily missions done. He is also focused on going to dungeons to level up. but he is still kind to the people that are important to him. Although Jin-Woo was shown to be indifferent when it to money, wealth and even humanly desires, he admitted to inadvertently took a peek at Hae-In's naked form after unwisely using 'Sensory Sharing' through the Shadow Soldier inserted into her shadow.

Background

Jin-Woo is the eldest child in his family. After his father, Il-Hwan disappeared inside an unknown gate, Jin-Woo took all the responsibilities for his sister, Jin-Ah and Kyung-He, his mother. He worked as an E-Ranked Hunter under the Korean Hunters Association for 4 years, then after the Double Dungeon incident, Jin-Woo became a Player and a Reawakened Hunter.

"I shall protect my family, even if that means every single Hunter in the world becomes my enemy" - Sung Jin-Woo
